Tubeamp

TubeAmp is a WebApp that creates Spotify playlists based on journeys on the London Underground. It can be invoked remotely by sending an SMS message to 07418 340 203 It can also be accessed at TubeAmp.me

A checkers game to play against an artificial intelligence that uses a Min-Max based approach. Although a very naive AI system, it was a very useful introduction to artificial intelligence systems.
